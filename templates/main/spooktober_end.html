As you enter the last tile the whole wall begins to slide into the floor. As the gap reaches 2 feet a black shape shoots through the opening and heads directly towards you. Instinctively you duck and cover your eyes. When nothing happens for a few seconds you open your eyes again.

Your eyes dart around the empty chamber, wondering where the black shape went. Before your concern can grow too large a low ruffling sound right next to you ear causes you to turn your head and see a large raven perched on your shoulder. It is almost weightless, which is remarkable given its size.

"You must be Quoth" you say, reaching out a hand. It gently nuzzles your index finger and gives what you assume to be a contented squawk. You look through the opening where the wall used to be for the first time and see a staircase leading up into the soft light of dusk. Slowly you ascend the stairs, wondering what adventure awaits you next.
